Documents must be signed by the party named in the court proceedings and lodged at the court where the matter commenced] 

 
You will need to [click this link](http://www.ucprforms.justice.nsw.gov.au) to download and complete the required forms - all forms are available from this link. 

To set aside the default judgment, you will need to download and complete: 

  *   Form 20 - Notice of motion
  *   Form 40 - Affidavit 

The notice of motion incurs a filing fee (except for small claims matters in the local court). The notice of motion will be listed in court to which you must attend; otherwise the motion will be dismissed. 

Once the notice of motion is granted, you will then be ordered to file a defence: 

  *   Form 7B - Defence - filing party acting in person or by authorised officer
